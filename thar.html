About Us

Off-Road Capability:

The Thar is designed for off-road adventures with features like four-wheel drive, high ground clearance, and robust suspension.

Classic Design:

The Thar's design pays homage to classic Jeep styling, featuring a boxy shape, removable doors, and a convertible soft-top.

Engine Options:

Mahindra Thar typically offers a range of engine options, including both petrol and diesel variants, providing customers with choices based on their preferences.

Interior Comfort:

Despite its rugged exterior, the Thar often comes equipped with modern interior features such as touchscreen infotainment systems, climate control, and comfortable seating.

Safety Features:

Standard safety features may include airbags, ABS (Anti-lock Braking System), and other safety technologies.
